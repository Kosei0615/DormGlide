# DormGlide New User Guide (Step-by-Step)

This guide is for first-time users who want to buy or sell quickly and safely.

## Quick Start (30 seconds)
1. Open DormGlide.
2. Sign in or create an account.
3. Browse listings and open an item.
4. Use Proceed to Purchase to start chat.
5. Confirm payment + meetup in chat.
6. Mark sold and confirm received.

---

## Step 1: Open the Website
What to do:
1. Open the DormGlide website link from your team.
2. Wait until the homepage finishes loading.

What you should see:
- Top navigation bar.
- Search area.
- Product cards with title, price, and status.

Visual cue:
[Logo] [Search bar] [Profile]
[Product Card] [Product Card] [Product Card]

---

## Step 2: Sign In or Create Account
What to do:
1. Tap Sign In.
2. Enter your account details.
3. Complete sign-in.

What you should see:
- A modal/popup for authentication.
- Your profile/menu options after login.

Visual cue:
[Auth Window]
Email: __________
Password: _______
[Sign In]

---

## Step 3: Find an Item
What to do:
1. Use search keywords (example: desk, textbook, monitor).
2. Apply filters if needed.
3. Open a listing you like.

What you should see:
- Matching listings update instantly.
- Sold items are labeled clearly.

Visual cue:
Search: [monitor                ]
Filters: [Category] [Price] [Condition]
Results: Available | Sold

---

## Step 4: Review Product Details
What to do:
1. Check title, price, description, and photos.
2. Confirm the item condition.
3. Verify seller info.

What you should see:
- Product detail page with big image and details.
- Action button: Proceed to Purchase (if available).

Visual cue:
[Photo]
Title: Monitor 24 inch
Price: $80
Status: Available
[Proceed to Purchase]

---

## Step 5: Start Purchase Chat
What to do:
1. Tap Proceed to Purchase.
2. Send your first message to seller.
3. Confirm item availability.

What you should see:
- Chat window opens.
- Optional starter text already filled in.

Visual cue:
[Chat]
Hi, I want to proceed with this purchase. Is it still available?
[Send]

---

## Step 6: Confirm Payment and Meetup (In Chat)
What to do:
1. Choose payment method: Cash, Zelle, or Venmo.
2. Confirm amount and meetup location/time.
3. Use safe meetup suggestions (public campus locations).

What you should see:
- Quick action buttons for templates.
- Safety reminder and meetup prompts.

Visual cue:
[Template: Proceed] [Template: Zelle] [Template: Venmo] [Template: Cash]
[Safe Meetup: Library Lobby] [Student Center]

---

## Step 7: Seller Marks Listing as Sold
What to do (seller):
1. After payment details are confirmed, click Seller: Confirm Sold.
2. Choose payment method used.
3. Save transaction.

What you should see:
- Status changes to Sold.
- Listing timeline updates.

Visual cue:
Status: Available -> Sold
[Seller: Confirm Sold]

---

## Step 8: Buyer Confirms Item Received
What to do (buyer):
1. After pickup, click Buyer: Confirm Received.
2. Submit confirmation.

What you should see:
- Buyer confirmation stored in timeline.
- Option to rate the other user.

Visual cue:
[Buyer: Confirm Received]
Timeline:
Requested -> Buyer Confirmed -> Seller Confirmed

---

## Step 9: Rate or Report if Needed
What to do:
1. If transaction is successful, leave a rating.
2. If there is an issue, use Problem with this transaction.
3. Describe the issue clearly.

What you should see:
- Rating prompt after confirmation.
- Issue report form flow in chat.

Visual cue:
[Rate User]
[Problem with this transaction]
Issue type: payment_issue / not_received / safety_concern

---

## Step 10: Manage Listings from Dashboard (Seller)
What to do:
1. Open your dashboard.
2. View your listing statuses.
3. Toggle Mark Sold or Mark Available when needed.

What you should see:
- Manage My Listings section.
- Mini timeline for each listing.

Visual cue:
[Manage My Listings]
Listing A | Status: Sold | [Mark Available]
Listing B | Status: Available | [Mark Sold]

---

## Safety Checklist (Share with all users)
1. Meet in public campus areas.
2. Prefer daytime meetup.
3. Confirm amount before meetup.
4. Keep payment screenshot/receipt until both sides confirm.
5. Use the report feature if something feels wrong.

---

## 60-Second Walkthrough Script (For Onboarding)
1. Open DormGlide and sign in.
2. Search for an item and open product details.
3. Click Proceed to Purchase and send first message.
4. Confirm payment method and meetup in chat.
5. Seller confirms sold, buyer confirms received.
6. Leave rating, or report issue if needed.

Done. New users can now complete a full buy/sell flow safely from start to finish.
