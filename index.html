About DormGlide

DormGlide is a modern marketplace platform designed specifically for college students to safely buy and sell used goods. Our mission is to create a trusted community where students can find affordable essentials and earn money from items they no longer need.

Founded by students, for students - we understand the unique needs of campus life.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Is DormGlide free to use?
    A: Yes! Creating an account and browsing items is completely free.
  • Q: How do I ensure safe transactions?
    A: We recommend meeting in public campus locations and using secure payment methods.
  • Q: Can only students use DormGlide?
    A: Currently, our platform is designed for college students with valid .edu email addresses.
  • Q: How do I report suspicious activity?
    A: Use our report feature or contact us directly through social media.
